# Privacy Policy for Cafey

**Effective Date:** April 2, 2026

This Privacy Policy applies to the Cafey application ("the App") for Android. The App was built with a strict "offline-first" and "zero-friction" philosophy. We believe your financial data is exclusively yours.

### 1. Data Collection and Storage
**We do not collect, store, or process any of your personal or financial data on any external servers.** All data you enter into Cafey—including transaction amounts, notes, categories, and recurring entries—is stored locally in a secure database directly on your device. 

### 2. Internet Access and Permissions
Cafey is designed to function entirely offline. The App does not request, nor does it possess, the Android `INTERNET` permission. Because it physically cannot connect to the internet, it is impossible for the App to transmit your data to us or any third party.

### 3. Third-Party Services and Analytics
We do not use any third-party analytics, tracking software, crash reporting tools, or advertising networks. Your usage habits and financial logs are entirely invisible to us.

### 4. Data Backups and Export
Cafey includes a feature that allows you to export your data to a CSV file. This process happens entirely locally on your device. You are solely responsible for where you store, send, or back up that exported file.

### 5. Changes to This Privacy Policy
If we ever change the core architecture of Cafey (for example, introducing an optional cloud-sync feature), we will update this Privacy Policy and notify you within the App before requesting any new permissions.

### 6. Contact Us
If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or how your data is handled, please contact us at: **contact@rioze.dev**
